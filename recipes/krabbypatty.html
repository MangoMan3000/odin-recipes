Krabby Patty

Description

The Krabby Patty is the Krusty Krab's signature food item. It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments and a patty.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Pickles
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Patty

Steps

  1. Shine the bun
  2. Grill the Patty
  3. Assemble in the following order:
    • Top bun
    • Pickles
    • Mustard
    • Ketchup
    • Tomatoes
    • Onions
    • Cheese
    • Lettuce
    • Patty
    • Bottom bun
  4. Enjoy
